RelaxVibe Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

RelaxVibe is designed with one fundamental principle: your relaxation is yours alone.

No Data Collection

RelaxVibe does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data on external servers. The app makes zero network requests. There is no analytics, no crash reporting services, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party libraries that communicate over the internet.

On-Device Processing

All relaxation is performed entirely on your device using an AI model that is bundled within the app itself. Your relaxation text is processed locally by this model and never leaves your phone. No internet connection is required to use any feature of the app.

Local Storage Only

Your relaxation journal entries (the text you enter and the AI-generated interpretations) are stored as local files in the app's sandboxed Documents directory on your device. This data is:

  • Not accessible to other apps
  • Not backed up to any cloud service by the app itself (standard iCloud device backups may include app data if enabled by the user in iOS Settings)
  • Permanently deleted when you delete the app

No Accounts

RelaxVibe does not require or offer user accounts, sign-ups, email addresses, or any form of authentication.

No Tracking

RelaxVibe does not use any tracking technologies. There are no cookies, no device fingerprinting, no advertising identifiers, and no usage analytics.

Children's Privacy

Since RelaxVibe collects no data whatsoever, it is inherently safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy is ever updated, changes will be noted here with a new "Last updated" date.
